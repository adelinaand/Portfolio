Sharing Expertise of Women for Peace

Sharing Expertise of Women for Peace (SEWP) is a tool created to facilitate women’s participation in peace processes, democratic processes and governance as well as pre-negotiation and negotiation phases. It is a visualization tool for finding specific knowledge in the places where it is needed, and broadening the group of people who are invited into peace negotiation situations. The visualization includes organisations, established activists, academics and researchers who work within peace effort, and shows where in the world they can be found. The aim of SEWP is to make it simple to find knowledge within the area of peace negotiation and the subjects related to that. It is a tool that is intended to be used internally in the UN and is therefore designed to fit in with the UN webpage to make it feel familiar and like a natural development of the already existing design.
